# Stay Safe Online Checklist



#### General Safety tips:



* Use strong, unique passwords for each account and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
* Do not click on unknown links received via email, SMS, or social media (common phishing method)
* Verify website URLs before entering login or personal details (look for HTTPS and correct spelling)
* Limit personal information shared online, especially on public profiles
* Download apps only from official sources like Play Store or App Store
* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities such as banking or logging into important accounts
* Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank
* Do not approve “collect requests” from unknown users on UPI apps (you may end up sending money instead of receiving it)
* Double-check recipient details before making any payment



#### Reporting Cybercrime:



* Report incidents immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
* Call the Cybercrime Helpline: 1930 (especially for financial fraud cases)
