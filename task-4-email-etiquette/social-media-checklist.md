\# Responsible Social Media Use – Checklist



✅ Do’s

* Think before posting – ensure your content is appropriate and respectful
* Maintain privacy settings – control who can view your personal information
* Verify information before sharing – avoid spreading misinformation
* Use a professional tone – especially when interacting with faculty or professionals
* Respect others online – engage politely and avoid offensive language



❌ Don’ts

* Don’t share sensitive personal information (address, phone number, passwords)
* Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content that could harm your reputation
* Don’t engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
* Don’t click on suspicious links or unknown sources
* Don’t overshare daily activities publicly, which can risk your safety
